In Loving Memory

Brad W. Halicki
3/19/1976 – 6/2/2025

About Brad

Brad W. Halicki was a loving, caring, and sweet man who lived with a brain condition since birth but never let it define him. Despite challenges, he remained generous, goofy, and encouraging, making everyone feel special. His spirit was infectious—and his warm “hey mum” always brought smiles.

  • Gardening—especially pulling weeds
  • Competitive at spoons card game
  • Fishing, boating, bowling, Steelers and baseball games
  • Avid football fan
  • Dog dad to Nemo & Choochie
  • Early riser, late nighter—always hungry
  • Favorite songs: *Celebration*, *I’m Too Sexy*, *It Wasn’t Me*, *My Girl*

  • Hated the news, loved his chair and the remote

  • Loved drive‑ins, music-in-the-park, and flirting
  • Cared deeply—loved buying dinner for loved ones
